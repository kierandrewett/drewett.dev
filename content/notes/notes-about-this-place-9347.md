date: 2026-05-20

I'm not going to call this a blog. Blogs feel like a commitment, and I'm not
making one. This is a place to drop short pieces of writing as they happen.

Slugs follow a simple pattern: `notes-about-something-NNNN`, where the four
digits are the last four of the Unix time when I wrote it. It's not for
sorting — it's just to keep the filenames unique without thinking about it.

Each note is a markdown file with frontmatter (`title`, `date`, `summary`),
fetched from the repo on the fly. Push a new file, wait a few minutes, and
it shows up. No rebuild, no deploy step.

That's pretty much it.
