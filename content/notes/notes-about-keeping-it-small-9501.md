---
title: Notes about keeping it small
date: 2026-05-15
summary: Most of the personal projects I keep around are deliberately under 1k lines.
---

The longer a side project is, the more time I spend reading it instead of
writing it. A thousand lines is roughly where I lose the whole thing in my
head and start needing notes-on-the-notes to remember what's where.

So I try to keep things small. If something has to grow, fine — but it
should earn the size, not get there by accident.
